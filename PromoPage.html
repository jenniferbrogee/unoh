Win $500 Grant for Your Startup!

Turn Your Idea Into Reality

Do you have a business idea you want to see come alive? Join fellow UNOH students in pitching your startup concept to faculty, alumni entrepreneurs, and local business leaders. The winning team receives a $500 grant to launch their business!

Important Dates

February 1, 2026

February 16, 2026
Team Selection - Selected teams will be notified
February 27, 2026
Slide Submission - Submit your presentation slides for review
March 6, 2026 (~11:00 AM)
Competition Day - Present your 10-minute pitch (in-person or remote)

Competition Details

Eligibility

  • All team members must be enrolled at UNOH
  • Students must be in good standing (2.0+ GPA, no holds or disciplinary actions)
  • Teams of 1-4 students

Presentation Format

  • 10-minute maximum pitch
  • Present in-person or remotely
  • Slides required for review
  • Judged on established criteria

The Judges

  • UNOH faculty members
  • Alumni award winners
  • Local business leaders
  • Independent & impartial

What Should Your Application Include?

Your Elevator Pitch
Describe your business in 1-2 compelling sentences
Problem & Solution
What problem are you solving and why does it matter?
Target Customers
Who will benefit from your product or service?
Market Research
What have you learned from customers or research?
Impact Vision
How will your idea change your community or industry?
Fund Usage Plan
How will you use the $500 grant if you win?

Important Information

Financial Aid: The grant may affect your financial aid. Contact the Financial Aid Office before accepting any prize.

Tax Implications: Winners must complete a W9 form as the prize is considered taxable income.

Confidentiality: You are responsible for protecting your proprietary ideas. UNOH takes no responsibility for unwanted disclosure.
